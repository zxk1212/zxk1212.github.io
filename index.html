About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Nanyang Ye. My research focuses on confidence calibration, latent diffusion model, vision language Model, etc. Prior to my doctoral studies, I received my bachelor's degree from Tianjin University, advised by Zongbo Han and Prof. Changqing Zhang.
