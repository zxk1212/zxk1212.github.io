About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Nanyang Ye. My work explores confidence calibration, latent diffusion models, and vision-language systems.

Before joining SJTU, I completed my B.E. in Artificial Intelligence at Tianjin University, where I was mentored by Zongbo Han and Prof. Changqing Zhang.
